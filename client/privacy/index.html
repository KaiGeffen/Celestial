Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 26, 2025

We're working hard to make Celestial the best game we can while working with our community members. We use analytics to ensure that we find and address the pain points where users get hung up, as well as giving you more of the things that you love.

We value transparency and want you to understand how we collect and use your information when you use our website and play our game.

1. Information We Collect

A. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our site. This may include:

  • Pages viewed
  • Time spent on site
  • Device/browser information
  • IP address (anonymized where applicable)

Google may process this information in accordance with their own Privacy Policy. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-Out Add-on.

B. Gameplay and User Data

We also collect and store the following data related to your gameplay activity and more, including:

  • Matches played

  • Decks created or used
  • Progress through quests or tutorials

  • Rankings or skill ratings
  • Player-chosen names and profiles

2. How We Use Your Data

We use this data to:

  • Improve game performance and balance
  • Diagnose bugs or abuse
  • Personalize your gameplay experience
  • Publish statistics, rankings, and match data

3. Public Disclosure of Gameplay Information

Certain gameplay data (such as match results, leaderboard rankings, or user profiles) may be made publicly available on our website or other services. This could include:

  • Your username
  • Your in-game performance
  • Your deck compositions or choices
  • Your progress or milestones

This helps foster community features like leaderboards, replays, and social sharing. By using our game, you consent to this data being shared publicly.

If you wish to opt out of public display of certain data, please contact us at [insert contact email].

4. Data Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. Outside of the public disclosures noted above, we may share information only with trusted services for hosting, analytics, or development.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to the data we store about you
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Request that certain data not be publicly displayed

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at tcgcelestial@gmail.com.

6. Cookies

We use cookies for site functionality and analytics. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7. Changes to This Policy

This policy may change over time. The latest version will always be available on our website with the effective date listed above.

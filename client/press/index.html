Description

Called to the mysterious City, travelers craft decks of 15 cards, each embodying their most cherished themes and profound lessons. Each match is a race to claim 5 rounds. During the day, players weave their cards into a shared story by spending breath. As night falls, these stories resolve, and the crowd awards the player with the highest points as victor.

From mastering the single-player Journey to testing your skills against others online, Celestial reimagines card games by fusing strategic depth with personal expression. With over 100 unique cards to collect and master, every match tells a different story.

Features

  • Perfect your card battling and deck-building skills in a unique hidden-information strategy game
  • Master the dynamic day and night cycle system to plan your next move and outmaneuver opponents
  • Build custom decks from over 100 unique cards, each representing themes and elements of personal journeys
  • Play cards face-down during the day phase, then reveal and resolve them at night for strategic depth
  • Complete challenges in the single-player Journey mode to unlock cards while learning about travelers called to the City
  • Compete against friends and rivals in online multiplayer matches
  • Play instantly in your web browser - no download required
  • Free to play with full access to all game features

History

Celestial Decks was born from a vision to create a card game that combines strategic depth with personal storytelling. The game's unique day and night cycle mechanic emerged from the desire to add psychological depth to traditional card game mechanics, where players must predict and counter their opponent's moves while building their own narrative.

Development focused on creating a game that could be learned in minutes but mastered over countless matches. The card collection grew organically, with each card designed to represent meaningful themes and lessons from personal journeys, making every deck a reflection of the player's choices and strategy.

Celestial Decks launched as a free-to-play web-based game, making it accessible to players everywhere without barriers. The game continues to evolve with new cards, features, and community-driven improvements.
